Artifact 01
PowerPoint Presentation

AI/ML History Visual Timeline

A professionally designed 5-slide PowerPoint presentation tracing the complete history of Artificial Intelligence — from Alan Turing's foundational 1950 paper through to the rise of ChatGPT and large language models in 2022.

The presentation covers the Dartmouth Conference of 1956 where AI was born as a field, both AI Winters caused by unmet expectations and funding cuts, the deep learning revolution driven by Geoffrey Hinton, Yann LeCun and Yoshua Bengio, and the transformer architecture that powers every modern AI system today.

Artifact 02
AI Chatbot Application

Change & AI Leadership Coach

A fully functional AI coaching chatbot built from scratch using the Google Gemini API and the complete five-stage Design Thinking methodology. The bot acts as an on-demand Socratic coaching assistant for AIML-500 students facing real AI adoption challenges.

Instead of giving direct answers, the bot first asks 1–2 clarifying questions to understand the situation — then coaches the user using recognised frameworks including ADKAR, Kotter's 8-Step Change Model, and CCL's 9 Leadership Competencies. Built as a single HTML file that opens in any browser with no installation needed.
